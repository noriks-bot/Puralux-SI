How it works.

Just add water? Let us explain how it works...

Ultrasonic Waves

High-frequency ultrasonic waves are generated in the water inside the Puralux Pod.

Cavitation (nano-bubbles)

This creates millions of nano-scale bubbles (called cavitation), which burst and release energy, removing plaque, bacteria and any other debris.

Bacterial breakdown

This release of high energy also breaks down cell membranes, destroying bacteria and protecting your oral health.

The Puralux Pod uses high-frequency 42,000 Hz ultrasonic waves to vibrate the solution and produce millions of nano-scale bubbles. These bubbles rapidly collapse, releasing a large amount of energy, breaking down bacteria and plaque while being non abrasive to your dental appliance.


Ultrasonic cleaning technology is safe and reliable and has been widely used in laboratory, commercial, industrial and other fields for decades.

The Puralux Pod has been specifically designed by our UK scientists to clean a wide range of oral appliances including all types of clear aligners, retainers (including those with metal), mouth guards, dentures, bite guards (night guards), sleep devices, toothbrush heads and more.

Read our blog here to learn more about what the Puralux Pod can clean. If you are still unsure, contact support to check if your specific appliance can be cleaned.

UV light isn’t effective when the appliance is sitting in dirty or cloudy water. The light can’t reach all surfaces because even very small amounts of debris in the water blocks it. Instead, the Puralux Pod uses best-in-class ultrasonic cleaning technology, which physically removes buildup and works reliably across the whole surface, even in hard to reach areas.
